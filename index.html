Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions About Our Services

What sets Elite Plumbing apart from other plumbing companies?
Elite Plumbing specializes in luxury plumbing solutions with an emphasis on craftsmanship, premium materials, and exceptional customer service. Our master plumbers have decades of experience working with high-end fixtures and designing custom plumbing systems for discerning homeowners.
Do you offer emergency plumbing services?
Yes, we offer emergency services to all our clients. Our Premium and Platinum package clients receive priority scheduling and dedicated emergency support.
How much does a typical plumbing service cost?
Costs vary based on the service. Basic services start at $150, while luxury renovations can range from $15,000 to $50,000+ depending on materials and scope. Request a quote for a detailed estimate.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Boise and surrounding areas within a 50-mile radius, including Meridian, Eagle, and Nampa.
How long does a bathroom renovation take?
A typical luxury bathroom renovation takes 4-8 weeks, depending on the complexity and customization required. We'll provide a timeline during your consultation.
Can you install smart home plumbing systems?
Yes, we specialize in smart home plumbing solutions, including automated water shut-off systems, smart water heaters, and integrated leak detection systems.
How do I know if I need a water filtration system?
If your water tastes off, has visible particles, or you're concerned about contaminants, a filtration system might be right for you. We can test your water during a consultation.
What should I do if my pipes are leaking?
Turn off your main water valve and call us immediately at (xxx) 007-0007 for emergency service.
Do you offer free consultations?
Yes, we provide free in-home consultations to assess your needs and discuss your project.
How often should I replace my plumbing fixtures?
High-quality fixtures can last 10-20 years with proper maintenance, but upgrading sooner can improve efficiency and aesthetics.
